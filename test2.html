About

We are not attempting to convey our political beliefs. Instead, we are attempting to disregard politics and purely focus on humanity. There comes a point where politics is not the solution, and rather, we should take a step back and examine our own ethics and morals -- in a word, humanity. Through this website, we hope to raise money and spread awareness on the issue to help undocumented immigrants get the rights they deserve.

This website was created under the collaborative efforts of Susan Dong, Wanying Du, Jocelin Guzman-Quintero, and Afia Saiyara. They are currently rising juniors and seniors who attend the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. Given the opportunity to create a website for their final project, they decided to focus on the conflicts concerning immigration to enhance their technical abilities and educate themselves further on the topic.
